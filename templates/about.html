Mission Statement

IvyTree provides a well-rounded approach to language learning with an emphasis on critical thinking through reading and writing. We guide students in the appropriate use of technology with cutting-edge, proven and emerging approaches to assist and develop their understanding of classic literature and universal writing skills.

Values

At IvyTree, we believe that education should exhibit these 6 features.

encourage curiosity

about the world and about communicating with others

foster imagination

by urging students to freely question ideas

promote risk-taking

by providing a safe environment

nurture critical thinking

empower

students to actively participate in their own learning process

respect

that students have individual learning styles and paces.
